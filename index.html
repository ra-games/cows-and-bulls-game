Cows and Bulls Game

Guess the 4-letter word!

Bulls: Correct letter in the correct position

Cows: Correct letter in the wrong position

How to Play?

1. The game selects a random 4-letter word from a predefined list.

2. The player inputs a 4-letter word as a guess.

3. The game provides feedback in the form of "Cows" and "Bulls".

Example: If the secret word is GAME and the guess is GEAR, the output would be 1 Bull (G) and 2 Cows (A and E).

4. The player keeps guessing based on the feedback until they find the correct word (4 Bulls).
